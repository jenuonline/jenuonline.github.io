Contact/Visit Us

We are a vibrant and spiritual community located in McKinney, dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. Our church is a place where people from all walks of life come together to worship, pray, and grow in their faith. We warmly invite you to join us for any of our services or events, where you'll experience the warmth of our fellowship and the beauty of our worship. Whether you are a lifelong Orthodox Christian or just starting to explore your faith, you are sure to find a home here at St. Paul's. We look forward to meeting you soon!
