Holy Consecration Celebration.

We are thrilled to invite you to the momentous occasion of consecrating and dedicating our beloved St. Paul's Orthodox Church on October 6th and 7th, 2023. This sacred event represents a significant milestone in our faith community's journey and symbolizes our commitment to the divine purpose of our cherished place of worship. St. Paul's Orthodox Church has long served as a spiritual home and beacon of light for our congregation, and this consecration marks a recommitment to our faith and the values of love, compassion, and kindness. Your presence at this event is eagerly anticipated as we embark on a renewed journey of faith, unity, and purpose under the guidance of H. H. Baselios Marthoma Mathews III, our esteemed chief celebrant. Together, let us gather in gratitude and reverence to consecrate this sacred space.
